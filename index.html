About

I'm Vikram Suthar, a 3D designer working across exterior and interior visualization. My focus is helping real estate professionals and homeowners see a space before it's built, furnished, or renovated — through photorealistic exterior renders, styled interior visuals, virtual staging of empty listings, and before/after virtual renovation concepts.

With 6 years of hands-on experience in 3D visualization, I've helped agents, builders, and homeowners turn an empty room or an unbuilt façade into something a buyer can actually picture living in — fast turnarounds, accurate lighting and materials, and a clean final image every time.
